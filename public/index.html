Lahaina Twin Project

The Lahaina Twin Project is an online platform that encourages public participation in the process of reconstructing Lahaina, Maui. It does this by offering forums, polls, and the sharing of design concepts together with data and visual models for analysis of the economy, ecology, etc. Its goal is to make it possible for a collaborative planning response to Lahaina's wildfire devastation. Our goal is for this effort to unite individuals in support of rebuilding Lahaina according to Maui's wishes.
